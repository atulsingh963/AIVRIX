# AIVRIX Logo Concept

## 1. Core Symbolism
The logo must reflect **Intelligence**, **Automation**, and **Forward-thinking Technology**.

### Concept A: The Neural Core
*   **Icon**: A stylized, geometric "A" that doubles as an abstract neural network node. Sharp edges on the outside to represent precision (software engineering), and a glowing, soft inner core to represent AI.
*   **Typography**: Custom geometric sans-serif. The "X" at the end should have a slight forward slant to indicate progress and speed.

### Concept B: The Infinite Loop
*   **Icon**: An abstract infinity symbol (`∞`) folded into the shape of an "A" or "V". Represents infinite possibilities, automation loops (DevOps/CI-CD), and continuous improvement.
*   **Typography**: Minimalist and highly tracked (widely spaced) font.

## 2. Technical Specifications
*   **Scalability**: The logo must look perfect as a 16x16px favicon and on a giant billboard.
*   **Variants**:
    *   Primary Logo (Icon + Wordmark) - Light & Dark modes.
    *   Logomark (Icon only) - Used for avatars, favicons.
    *   Wordmark (Text only) - Used where vertical space is constrained.
*   **Color**: The primary logomark should utilize the primary Electric Indigo (`#4F46E5`) to Neon Cyan (`#06B6D4`) gradient.
