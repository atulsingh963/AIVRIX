# AIVRIX Brand Identity

## 1. Brand Heart
*   **Name**: AIVRIX (A-I-V-R-I-X)
*   **Mission**: Helping businesses transform through Artificial Intelligence, Automation, Cloud Technologies, and Custom Software.
*   **Vision**: Become one of the leading AI Engineering and Software Development companies delivering enterprise-grade digital solutions.

## 2. Brand Personality & Tone
*   **Personality**: Premium, Modern, Intelligent, Innovative, Minimal, Professional, Fast, Reliable.
*   **Tone of Voice**: Authoritative yet accessible. Forward-thinking and confident. We don't just write code; we engineer the future.

## 3. Visual Identity Guidelines
AIVRIX's visual identity draws inspiration from industry leaders like Stripe, Vercel, Linear, and Framer.

### 3.1 Color Palette
*   **Primary Brand Color**: Electric Indigo (`#4F46E5` / Tailwind `indigo-600`) - Represents technology, intelligence, and modern SaaS.
*   **Secondary/Accent**: Neon Cyan (`#06B6D4` / Tailwind `cyan-500`) - Used for gradients and highlighting AI features.
*   **Background (Dark Mode)**: Pure Black (`#000000`) and Deep Slate (`#09090B`).
*   **Background (Light Mode)**: Pristine White (`#FFFFFF`) and Subtle Gray (`#FAFAFA`).
*   **Text Colors**:
    *   Dark Mode: Off-white (`#EDEDED`), Muted Gray (`#A1A1AA`).
    *   Light Mode: Near Black (`#171717`), Muted Gray (`#52525B`).

### 3.2 Typography
*   **Headings**: `Inter` or `Geist` - Clean, geometric, and modern. Tight tracking for a premium feel.
*   **Body Text**: `Inter` - Highly legible at all sizes.
*   **Code Snippets/Technical Data**: `JetBrains Mono` or `Fira Code`.

### 3.3 UI Elements & Styling
*   **Glassmorphism**: Subtle frosted glass effects for navigation bars, cards, and modals (`backdrop-blur-md` with semi-transparent backgrounds).
*   **Borders**: Extremely subtle, 1px borders (`border-white/10` in dark mode) to define edges without clutter.
*   **Gradients**: Smooth, mesh gradients used sparingly for hero sections and primary call-to-action buttons.
*   **Animations**: Micro-interactions on hover, smooth page transitions, and subtle reveal animations on scroll (via Framer Motion and GSAP).

## 4. Imagery & Illustrations
*   Avoid standard stock photos. Use abstract 3D shapes, sleek dashboard mockups, and subtle geometric patterns.
*   Use high-contrast visuals to make the UI pop against the dark mode background.
